Transparency

A Note on Our Russia Placements

We understand that Russia placements raise legitimate questions. We want to be completely transparent about what we do — and what we absolutely do not do.

✅

Civil & Commercial Only

All our Russia placements are strictly within the civilian corridor — construction, agriculture, infrastructure, and industrial projects. Legitimate work, legitimate employers, legitimate contracts.

🚫

No Military. No Frontline. Ever.

We have zero involvement with the military, defence, or any security-related industry. We do not place workers in conflict zones, frontline regions, or anywhere near active military operations.

🚫

No Defence Industry

We do not place workers in weapons manufacturing, drone factories, munitions, or any defence-related facility. If it is connected to the military-industrial complex in any way, we do not touch it.

✅

Your Safety is Non-Negotiable

Every placement is vetted for worker safety and legal compliance. You go to work, earn well, and come home. That is our only mandate. Your family's peace of mind matters to us as much as it does to you.

Our commitment: Prodesse Manpower operates with the same ethical standards that have defined Prodesse Health for years. We will never compromise the safety, dignity, or integrity of any worker we represent — in Russia, the GCC, or anywhere else in the world.

Our clients hold the same values. Every employer we work with is carefully vetted to ensure they share our ethical standards. We only partner with civilian businesses that are wholly unconnected to the war industry, the defence sector, or any industry that directly or indirectly supports it. If a client does not meet that standard, we do not work with them — full stop.
